How Staff Should Complete a Pest Sighting Entry:
  1. When you observe any pest, fill out the white "Staff Report" row immediately. Record the date, time, location code, pest type code, your name, and a brief description of what you saw.
  2. Notify your supervisor and the Facility IPM Coordinator right away.
  3. Do not apply any pesticide or attempt to treat the area yourself.
  4. The Pest Pro technician will complete the shaded "Tech Response" row when they respond.
  5. All entries must be signed — no anonymous reports accepted.

⚠️ Urgent / Critical Pest Situations (bed bugs, rodents, or stinging/biting insects in patient areas): Notify supervisor and IPM Coordinator immediately. Isolate the area if feasible. Call Pest Pro directly: (407) 922-2276 · David Kell: (407) 840-8852.

Integrated Pest Management (IPM) Policy — Rehabilitation Center of Orlando

This facility maintains an Integrated Pest Management program in partnership with Pest Pro, LLC. IPM prioritizes prevention, monitoring, and documentation to protect patient health, staff safety, and regulatory compliance.


Core Principles:

  • Prevention first: Sanitation, structural exclusion, and elimination of harborage sites are the primary defense.
  • Staff involvement: All staff must report pest sightings using this log — no sighting is too minor. Logs are maintained at the Reception Area.
  • No self-treatment: Staff must not apply any pesticide or pest control product. All treatment is performed by licensed Pest Pro technicians only.
  • Documentation: All sightings and responses are logged in this binder. Pesticide application records are maintained digitally via FieldworkHQ and Excelsior's work order systems. All records available to AHCA inspectors upon request.
  • Communication: Pest Pro will notify the IPM Coordinator of significant findings, recommended facility actions, or required follow-up.

Staff Responsibilities Guide

  • Report any pest sighting immediately in this log
  • Notify supervisor and IPM Coordinator right away
  • Do NOT apply sprays, traps, or any pesticide
  • Do not move or disturb pests or evidence
  • For bed bugs, rodents, or stinging/biting insects in patient areas: isolate room, notify charge nurse, call Pest Pro directly: (407) 922-2276 or David Kell: (407) 840-8852
  • Maintain clean, dry, clutter-free work areas

Maintenance Responsibilities Guide

  • Maintain this binder — current, organized, accessible at Reception
  • Coordinate with Pest Pro for weekly service visits
  • Ensure access to all service areas during technician visits
  • Complete facility-side recommendations promptly
  • Maintain door sweeps, screens, and structural exclusion
  • Manage trash removal frequency and container conditions
  • Transfer completed logs to Maintenance Office for storage
