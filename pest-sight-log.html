How to Complete a Pest Sighting Entry (Staff Instructions):
  1. When you observe any pest, fill out the gray "Staff Report" row immediately. Record the date, time, location, pest type, and your name. Describe what you saw (live/dead, quantity, where exactly).
  2. Contact the Facility IPM Coordinator or Maintenance immediately after completing the entry.
  3. Do not apply any pesticide or attempt to treat the area yourself. Use approved sanitation protocols only.
  4. The Pest Pro technician will complete the yellow "Tech Response" row when they respond.
  5. All entries must be signed. No anonymous reports.
Urgent / Critical Pest Situations: Bed bugs or rodents in patient areas → notify Supervisor and IPM Coordinator immediately, isolate area if feasible, call Pest Pro directly at (407) 922-2276.

Integrated Pest Management (IPM) Policy — Rehabilitation Center of Orlando

This facility is committed to a proactive, Integrated Pest Management approach to pest control in partnership with Pest Pro, LLC. IPM prioritizes prevention, monitoring, and documentation over reactive chemical treatment, with the goal of protecting patient health, staff safety, and regulatory compliance.


Core IPM Principles at this facility:

  • Prevention first: Sanitation, structural exclusion, and elimination of harborage sites are the first line of defense.
  • Staff involvement: All staff are expected to report pest sightings using this log. No sighting is too minor to report.
  • No self-treatment: Staff must not apply any pesticide or pest control product. All treatment is performed by licensed Pest Pro technicians only.
  • Documentation: All sightings and technician responses are logged in this binder, which is maintained in the Maintenance Department and available to AHCA inspectors at all times.
  • Communication: Pest Pro will notify the facility IPM Coordinator of any significant findings, recommended facility-side actions, or follow-up required.

Staff Responsibilities — Quick Reference

  • Report any pest sighting immediately
  • Complete the Staff row in the Pest Sighting Log
  • Notify supervisor and IPM Coordinator
  • Do NOT apply sprays, traps, or any pesticide
  • Do not move or disturb pests or evidence
  • For bed bugs or rodents in patient areas: isolate room, notify charge nurse, call Pest Pro directly at (407) 922-2276
  • Maintain clean, dry, clutter-free work areas
  • Report sanitation or structural issues (leaks, gaps, etc.) to Maintenance

Maintenance Responsibilities

  • Maintain this binder — current, organized, available
  • Coordinate with Pest Pro for scheduled visits
  • Ensure access to all service areas during technician visits
  • Complete facility-side recommendations within reasonable timeframe
  • Maintain door seals, screens, and structural exclusion
  • Manage trash/waste removal frequency and container condition
  • Ensure kitchen/dining sanitation standards are maintained
  • Contact Pest Pro for any urgent / between-service sightings
